About

Who I Am

I'm an AI Engineer with deep expertise in medical data processing and intelligent clinical systems. My work spans multimodal medical imaging — CT, ultrasound, radiography, and echocardiography — as well as wearable biosignal analysis, building end-to-end AI pipelines from research to production deployment.

With a background in Biomedical Engineering and experience across Tehran, Yerevan, and international research competitions, I bridge the gap between academic research and real-world clinical AI. I've delivered deep learning systems for orthodontic treatment planning, colonoscopy polyp detection, pulmonary embolism segmentation, fetal biometry, and real-time ECG/PPG analysis.

My current focus is augmented reality applications in 3D medical image processing and volumetric visualization, developing next-generation interfaces for clinical decision support and immersive diagnostic workflows.
